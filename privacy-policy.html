Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Our apps may collect the following types of information:

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve our games
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob

Third-Party Advertising

Our apps use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings.

Data Retention

We retain collected data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are rated for Everyone and do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.
